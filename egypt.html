Architecture
Archictural Mastery

The pyramid was orignally built at 146.6 meters tall, with an orignal casing of highly polished white limestone meant to reflect the sun's rays and make the pyramid shine like a jewel

Explore Inside

The interior chambers of the pyramid are open to visitors, including the Queen's Chamber, The Grand Gallery and the awe inspiring King's Chamber with its granite sarcophagus
